# Company Logos

This directory should contain company logo files for the "Companies I've Worked With" section.

## Required Logo Files

Please add the following logo files (SVG or PNG format):

1. `fidelity.svg` or `fidelity.png` - Fidelity Investments logo
2. `nike.svg` or `nike.png` - Nike logo
3. `apple.svg` or `apple.png` - Apple logo
4. `vanguard.svg` or `vanguard.png` - Vanguard logo
5. `capital-one.svg` or `capital-one.png` - Capital One logo
6. `unt.svg` or `unt.png` - University of North Texas logo
7. `invesco.svg` or `invesco.png` - Invesco logo

## Logo Specifications

- **Format**: SVG preferred (scalable), PNG acceptable
- **Size**: Approximately 120x60px or similar aspect ratio
- **Background**: Transparent
- **Style**: Official company logos (can be found on company websites or logo repositories)
- **Color**: Full color (CSS grayscale filter will be applied automatically)

## Where to Find Logos

- Company official websites (usually in "Press" or "Brand" sections)
- [Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/)
- [Brandfetch](https://brandfetch.com/)
- [Clearbit Logo API](https://clearbit.com/logo)

## Temporary Placeholders

Until actual logos are added, the Companies section will show broken image placeholders or you can use text-based fallbacks.
